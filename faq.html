Ordering & Delivery

6 questions
How do I place an order?
Browse products, add to cart, checkout via WhatsApp or website. We'll confirm your order within 2 hours.
How long does delivery take?
Colombo: 1-2 business days. Suburbs: 2-3 days. Outstation: 3-5 business days.
Do you deliver island-wide?
Yes! We deliver to all 25 districts across Sri Lanka.
Can I track my order?
Yes, you'll receive a tracking link via WhatsApp once your order ships.
Is there a minimum order amount?
No minimum. However, orders above Rs. 5,000 get free delivery.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
Same-day delivery available in Colombo for orders placed before 12pm.

Payments

4 questions
What payment methods do you accept?
Cash on Delivery (COD), bank transfer, Visa/Mastercard via link payment.
Do I pay before delivery?
Only if you choose bank transfer or card. With COD, you pay when the item arrives.
Is cash on delivery really free?
Yes, there's no extra charge for COD.
Can I pay in installments?
Not currently, but we're working on it.

Returns & Refunds

5 questions
What is your return policy?
7-day no-questions-asked returns on all products. Item must be unused and in original packaging.
How do I return an item?
WhatsApp us at +94 77 767 5984 with your order number. We'll arrange a free pickup.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item.
Can I exchange instead of return?
Yes! Just tell us what you'd like instead when you contact us.
What if my item arrives damaged?
Send us photos on WhatsApp within 24 hours. We'll send a free replacement immediately.

Products

4 questions
Are your products genuine?
Every product is sourced directly and inspected before shipping. We guarantee quality.
Do you have warranty?
Yes, 30-day warranty on all items against manufacturing defects.
Can I see products before buying?
Visit our showroom at Maliban Street, Colombo 11, open 7 days 10am-7pm.
Do you do wholesale?
Yes! WhatsApp us for wholesale pricing on bulk orders.

Account & Support
