Support

Common questions

Quick answers to the things people ask us most.

How long does delivery take?
Delivery within Colombo takes 1–2 business days. For other areas across Sri Lanka, expect 3–5 business days. You'll receive a tracking update via WhatsApp once your order is dispatched.
Can I return a product?
Yes. We offer a 7-day return policy on all items. If the product is damaged, defective, or not what you expected, message us on WhatsApp with your order number and photos. We'll arrange a pickup and full refund.
Do you deliver to my area?
We deliver island-wide across all 25 districts of Sri Lanka. Whether you're in Jaffna, Galle, Kandy, or anywhere in between, we've got you covered. Delivery fees vary by location and are shown at checkout.
How does cash on delivery work?
Simply place your order online and choose Cash on Delivery at checkout. When the courier arrives at your door with the package, you pay the total amount in cash. No card or online payment needed. You only pay when you have the product in your hands.
