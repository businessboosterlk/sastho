How we're different

Three promises that set us apart from every other store.

Direct sourcing

We cut the middleman entirely, passing those savings directly to you. Factory to your door, nothing in between.

Cash on delivery

Zero risk. You pay only when you see the product, hold it in your hands, and love it. No card, no advance payments.

Quality promise

Every single product is personally inspected before it leaves our warehouse. If it's not perfect, it doesn't ship.

What we stand for

01

Honesty in pricing

We don't inflate prices to create fake discounts. What you see is a fair price, every day. Our margins are thin because we'd rather earn your trust than a few extra rupees.

02

Quality over quantity

We'd rather stock 200 products we're proud of than 2,000 we're not sure about. Every item in our catalog has been tested, used, and approved by our own team first.

Customer first, always
